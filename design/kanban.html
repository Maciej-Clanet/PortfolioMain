Project Overview

In order to learn more about collaborative environments, I have designed this "Kanban for Teams" with the intention of later developing it and including features such as:

  • Real-time collaboration on a board
  • Creating teams and managing members
  • Configurable access settings for boards
  • Setting boards as favorites
  • Folder organization

The current design showcases a lot of this, although it's not fully complete. Many of the settings menus have not been designed yet. Nevertheless, I think what's there showcases the general idea.
